Our Journey

From humble beginnings to industry leadership

1990
Foundation

Founded by master craftsman Rajesh Sharma in a small workshop in Jaipur with a vision for quality furniture.

1995
First Showroom

Expanded to our first retail showroom in Delhi, introducing contemporary designs alongside traditional pieces.

2005
Global Reach

Launched international exports to Europe and North America, sharing Indian craftsmanship worldwide.

The Odejia Difference

We handpick sustainably sourced hardwoods, allowing them to acclimate naturally before crafting begins.

Using time-honored techniques that create stronger, longer-lasting furniture without relying on modern shortcuts.

Multiple layers of natural finishes applied by hand for depth, character, and lasting protection.
